# Privacy Policy for Finshyt

**Last Updated: March 2, 2026**

### 1. Introduction
Finshyt ("we," "our," or "the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your data. The App is designed as a "privacy-first" tool, meaning your financial data stays under your control on your own device.

### 2. Information Collection and Use
To provide automated expense tracking, the App requires access to sensitive information. We only process this data locally on your device:
* **SMS Data:** The App reads incoming SMS messages to detect transaction alerts from banks and payment providers.
* **Notifications:** The App monitors system notifications to identify successful payment alerts from UPI and banking applications.
* **Accessibility Services:** With your explicit consent, we use Accessibility Services to detect payment success screens within other apps to provide precise transaction logging.
* **Transaction Details:** We extract amounts, merchant names, and timestamps to create your financial dashboard.

### 3. Data Storage (Local-Only)
* **No Cloud Sync:** All detected transactions and financial insights are stored exclusively in your device's local storage.
* **User Control:** Because data is stored locally, deleting the App or clearing App data will permanently remove your transaction history. We do not maintain backups on any external servers.

### 4. Data Sharing
We **do not** sell, trade, or share your personal or financial data with third parties. No data is transmitted off your device.

### 5. Permissions Required
* **SMS:** To parse bank alerts.
* **Notifications:** To detect payment app alerts.
* **Accessibility:** To log transactions from payment success screens.
